The Activity

The Technomancers worked with Radioshack on Jan. 28, 2017, during Radioshack's Build-A-Drone event. Members of the team helped to supervise and build drones with participants of the activity, sharing valuable knowledge, skills, and experiences with different people. The Technomancers hope to cooperate with Radioshack further around the event and in the near future, such as by holding exhibitions near the store to increase the store's and the team's popularity, allowing people to drive our robots in safe environments.
