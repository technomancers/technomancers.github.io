The Technomancers

From our beginning in 2005, FIRST Team 1758 has enjoyed the support provided by Florence School District One (Florence, SC). FSD1 has provided funding, facilities, and teacher support along with students from the three District One high schools:

  • South Florence High School
  • West Florence High School
  • Wilson High School

Our home is in space provided by Principal Al Bradley of the Florence Career Center, which gives the space, tools, and equipment needed to develop our students' design, build, and organizational skills. In addition, General/Electric (GE) has also provided funding and volunteer mentors.

COMPETITION

We have competed in FRC regionals since 2005, winning the Palmetto Regional in 2008 and reaching the semis in 2010, 2012, and 2014.

KNOWLEDGE IS POWER

The Technomancers encourages hands on participation from its members, with experiences ranging from welding, electrical wiring, programming, mechanical design, and more.

CONNECTIONS

Members of team 1758 have gone on to major in STEM fields, with colleges such as Georgia Tech, Brown, Duke, UPenn, MIT, Berkeley, and Clemson.
