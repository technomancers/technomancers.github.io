    Teen Tech Week

Library Demonstrations

Team 1758 The Technomancers will be hosted by the Florence County Library to celebrate Teen Tech Week. On Saturday, March 11, the Technomancers will host demonstrations and exhibitions of our robot and discuss robotics with anyone interested from 2:00 P.M. to 4:00 P.M. Members of the team will talk to people about the team and how one can join or help the team. The event is open to all students in 6th to 12th grade and their families.
