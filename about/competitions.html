    Our Competitions

Palmetto Regional

Team 1758 the Technomancers attends the Palmetto Regional, held in Myrtle Beach, SC, every year since 2005. We won the Palmetto Regional in 2008, and we reached the semifinals in 2010, 2012, and 2014.

Other Competitions

Every year, the Technomancers attends another regional competition. Recently, we've attended competitions in West Palm Beach, Florida, and Richmond, Virginia. This year, we plan to attend the regional in Huntsville, Alabama.

SCRIW

Every year, the team attends a scrimmage competition between build seasons, SCRIW. SCRIW is held in Columbia, South Carolina, at Dreher High School.
